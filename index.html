Volkswagen driverless car

Volkswagen is testing its autonomous ID BUZZ AD prototype on public roads for the first time. The German automaker took the Level 4 electric vehicle for a test run on the streets of Munich, with VW Group CEO Herbert Deiss as a passenger.
